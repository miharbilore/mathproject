  \item \small A medical dosage requires $15$ pills to be divided among $5$ patients. How many pills will each patient receive, with a remainder of $0$?

    \item $3$
    \item $4$
    \item $5$
    \item $2$
    \item $1$
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small A cell grows at a rate of $24$ new cells per hour. If the cell growth is divided into $4$ periods, how many new cells will each period have, with a remainder of $0$?

    \item $6$
    \item $8$
    \item $12$
    \item $4$
    \item $2$
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small A hospital has $36$ beds in a ward. If the beds are divided into $9$ rooms, how many beds will each room have, with a remainder of $0$?

    \item $4$
    \item $5$
    \item $6$
    \item $3$
    \item $2$
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small What is the remainder when $53$ is divided by $5$?

    \item $4$
    \item $0$
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small What is the quotient when $48$ is divided by $6$?

  \end{enumerate}
