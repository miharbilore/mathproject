  \item \small A music producer is creating a rhythm pattern with $12$ beats. If the pattern is divided into groups of $4$ beats, what is the remainder?

    \item 0
    \item 1
    \item 2
    \item 3
    \item 4
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small A painting has a canvas size of $45$ inches by $30$ inches. If the painter wants to divide the canvas into equal sections with a width of $5$ inches, what is the remainder of the division of the length?
  \begin{enumerate}[label=(\Alph*), leftmargin=0.6cm, nosep, itemsep=2pt]
    \item 0
    \item 1
    \item 2
    \item 3
    \item 4
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small A frequency of $36$ Hz is divided by $4$ Hz. What is the quotient and remainder?
  \begin{enumerate}[label=(\Alph*), leftmargin=0.6cm, nosep, itemsep=2pt]
    \item $9$ R $0$
    \item $8$ R $2$
    \item $7$ R $3$
    \item $6$ R $4$
    \item $5$ R $5$
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small The number $48$ is divisible by $2$, $3$, and $5$. What is the smallest number that is divisible by $2$, $3$, $5$, and $9$?

    \item 90
    \item 180
    \item 270
    \item 360
    \item 450
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small A song has $63$ beats per minute. If the song is divided into sections of $7$ beats, what is the remainder?
  \begin{enumerate}[label=(\Alph*), leftmargin=0.6cm, nosep, itemsep=2pt]
    \item 0
    \item 1
    \item 2
    \item 3
    \item 4
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small The sum of the digits of $216$ is $9$. What is the largest number less than $1000$ that has a digit sum of $9$?

    \item 900
    \item 810
    \item 729
    \item 630
    \item 540
  \end{enumerate}

  \item \small A painter is mixing colors with frequencies of $24$ Hz, $30$ Hz, and $36$ Hz. What is the greatest common divisor of these frequencies?

    \item 2
    \item 3
    \item 6
    \item 12
